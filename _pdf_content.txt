Requirement Doc: Account
Deletion Page for JOL Puzzles
1. Overview
As part of the Google Play Data Safety requirements, we must provide a publicly
accessible URL where users can request the deletion of their account and all
associated data. This link will be displayed on the Google Play Store listing.
2. Page Requirements
The webpage must clearly state the following information to pass Google's review
process:
A. App Identification
The page must mention the app name: JOL Puzzles.
B. Account Deletion Steps
Users must be given clear instructions on how to delete their account.
In-App Path: "Open JOL Puzzles, navigate to Settings > Account > Delete
Account."
Alternative Path (Manual Request): "To request account deletion without the app,
please email our support team at [Support Email Address] with your
username/email. We will verify your identity before processing the request."
C. Data Retention & Deletion Policy
What is Deleted: User profile (name, email, avatar), game scores, wallet balance,
and referral history.
What is Kept: We may retain anonymized data for analytical purposes or specific
transaction records if required by regional financial laws.
Timeline: Deletion requests should be processed within 30 days.
3. Technical Specs
URL Structure Recommended: https://yourdomain.com/jolpuzzles/delete-account
Accessibility: The page must be publicly accessible without requiring a login (so
users who uninstalled the app can still find it).

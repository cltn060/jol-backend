Delete Your Account

Learn how to delete your JOL Puzzles account and understand what happens to your data.

📱

Delete From the App

Open JOL Puzzles and follow this path:

Settings → Account → Delete Account

Follow the on-screen prompts to confirm. Your deletion request will be processed immediately.

✉️

Request Deletion via Email

If you no longer have access to the app, you can request account deletion by emailing our support team at jolpuzzles.info@gmail.com.

Please include your username or registered email address. We will verify your identity before processing the request.

🗃️

What Happens to Your Data

Permanently deleted
  • User profile (name, email, avatar)
  • Game scores & progress
  • Wallet balance
  • Referral history
May be retained
  • Anonymized data for analytical purposes
  • Transaction records required by regional financial laws
⏱️

Processing Timeline

All deletion requests are processed within:

🕐 30 Days

You will receive a confirmation email once the process is complete.
